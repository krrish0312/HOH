Activity 1: Elderly Assistance

Connect with elderly individuals in our community through regular visits, phone calls, or platforms like 1 Letter 1 Smile, where we can share stories, memories, and kindness to help alleviate loneliness and bring joy to their lives.

Activity 2: Distribution of Hygiene Kits

Organize fundraising campaigns to collect essential items like toiletries, towels, and clothing. Assemble and distribute hygiene kits to local shelters, food banks, or community organizations, promoting health, and well-being for vulnerable populations.

Activity 3: Water Conservation Drive

Join us for our Water Conservation Drive to protect our most precious resource! This initiative aims to raise awareness about the importance of saving water and implementing practical strategies for conservation in our community.
